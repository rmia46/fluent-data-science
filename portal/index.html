How to Learn

📚 The Learning Workflow

All training materials are officially accessible via this portal. Each module is designed as an architectural guide for self-paced study:

PDF Materials

Shortened topic introductions to give you the foundational concept.

⚠️ Note: These PDFs do not contain in-depth knowledge. You are expected to use AI (like Gemini or ChatGPT) or documentation to study these concepts deeply.

You may use the rMentor Gemini Gem for guided learning through these topics.

Theory & Practice Notebooks

  • Theory (.ipynb): Contain core coding explanations and completed solutions for reference.
  • Practice (.ipynb): Your hands-on coding playground. Open these directly in Google Colab, Kaggle, or Marimo (Local) to write and test your code. Marimo uses .py for their notebooks.

💡 The Rigorous Practice Rule

Default notebooks are never enough for mastery.

"Create a new notebook, prompt an AI to generate a custom problem sheet for today's topic, and solve it until the logic is muscle memory."

📂 Real-World Practice

Mimic industry data science workflows.

"Avoid practicing with hardcoded arrays. Instead, work directly with .csv files. Ask your AI agent to generate suitable .csv datasets for the topic you are learning."
