Course Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive curriculum designed for competitive data science. We cover the entire pipeline from mathematical foundations to production-grade model ensembling.

Modules

  • 01 Mathematics for ML: Linear Algebra, Calculus, Statistics.
  • 02 Tabular Foundations: High-speed Pandas & NumPy.
  • 03 Feature Engineering I: Encoding, Scaling, Binning.
  • 04 Feature Engineering II: Golden Features & OOF Encoding.
  • 05 Validation Architecture: Cross-Validation & Leakage.
  • 06 Tree-Based Models: LightGBM, XGBoost, CatBoost.
  • 07 Optimization & Ensembling: Optuna & Blending.
  • 08 Production Pipelines: Scaling and Deployment.
  • 09 Mock Competition: The Final Churn Challenge.
