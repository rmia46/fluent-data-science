1. Vectorization: The Loop Ban

Why do we need this?

Python loops are too slow for millions of rows. Vectorization performs parallel operations at the C level.

Intuition: The Assembly Line

Imagine a loop as one worker picking up one box. Vectorization is an assembly line processing 1,000 boxes at once.

Test Your Understanding

How much faster is vectorization than .iterrows() on 1M rows?

Answer: Usually 100x to 1000x faster.

Learning Outcome

  • ✓ Eliminate loops from your data prep.
