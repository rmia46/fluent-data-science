Module 03

Feature Engineering I

The art of transforming raw data into "Golden Features" that models can actually understand.

1. Categorical Encoding

Why do we need this?

Models cannot process words. You must convert text into numbers without losing structural information.

Intuition: The Color Palette

OHE gives each color its own bucket. Label Encoding gives them a number on a scale.

Test Your Understanding

Which encoding is best for [Cold, Warm, Hot]?

Answer: Ordinal Encoding.

Learning Outcome

  • ✓ Decide between OHE and Frequency encoding.
  • ✓ Tame skewness with Log Transforms.
