9-Day Hyper-Drive Plan

The ultimate strategy for competitive data science contests.

Duration

9 Days

Daily Rigor

9 Hours

Session Split

1hr Theory / 2hr Lab

Target

Tabular ML

Day 01: Mathematics for ML

1. Linear Algebra

Vectors, matrices, dot products, and dimensions. Eigenvalues and PCA foundations.

2. Calculus

Derivatives, partials, and the chain rule. Gradient Descent intuition and optimization.

3. Probability

Distributions, Bayes' Theorem, and Z-scores. Standardizing features for model stability.

Day 02: Tabular Foundations

1. High-Speed Pandas

Vectorized operations and the 'Loop Ban'. Handling millions of rows efficiently.

2. Relational Ops

Mastering groupbys, complex merges, and structural joins across multi-table datasets.

3. Diagnostic Cleaning

Imputation vs. missingness indicator flags. Separating signal from noise in messy data.

Day 03: Feature Engineering I

1. Categorical Coding

OHE vs. Label vs. Frequency Encoding. Managing high-cardinality features.

2. Numerical Scaling

Skewness correction, log transforms, and Scaling choices (Z-score, MinMax).

3. Interactions

Constructing ratios, products, and continuous binning to expose hidden patterns.

Day 04: Feature Engineering II

1. Target Encoding

Advanced OOF Target Encoding with smoothing. Handling ID columns without leakage.

2. Contextual Transforms

Using .transform() to create peer-group metrics and localized behavioral signals.

3. Selection/Pruning

Dropping collinear features and using LightGBM importance to rank predictors.

Day 05: Validation Architecture

1. CV Frameworks

Stratified K-Fold vs. Time-Series Splits. Building the 'Truth' pipeline.

2. Baseline Models

Training RF and Linear baselines within the CV loop for stable benchmarks.

3. Leakage Auditing

Hunting silent performance inflation. Ensuring transformations stay inside folds.

Day 06: Tree-Based Models

1. LightGBM & XGB

Mastering GBDT mechanics. Handling missing values and categorical parameters.

2. Overfitting Analysis

Mapping the divergence between Train and Val scores. Tuning depth and leaves.

3. Regularization

Taming variance with subsampling, colsampling, and L1/L2 penalties.

Day 07: Optimization & Ensembling

1. Optuna Tuning

Automated Bayesian search for hyperparameter optimization in high-dimensional spaces.

2. Blending

Ensembling low-correlation models (LGBM + XGB) via weighted prediction averaging.

3. Metric Optimization

Post-processing. Finding the optimal probability threshold to maximize F1/MCC.

Day 08: Production Pipelines

1. Modular Code

Architecting clean pipelines with modular Python scripts (Prep, Model, Main).

2. Collaborative Git

Handling PRs, branching, and merge conflicts under competition stress.

3. Master Template

Building a ready-to-run template for instant deployment in any datathon.

Day 09: 4-Hour Mock Competition

Apply everything. A raw dataset, a hidden test set, and a ticking clock. Your mission is to iterate, validate, and blend your way to the top of the leaderboard.
